Our Culture!

CultureAct is a charity organization on a mission to drive and foster interaction across communities enabling the advancement of education and promote awareness of arts, culture, and heritage. We know there are “Chinese walls” across cultures and communities. However, in the world we live in today to empower the next generation to be good global citizens, we have to be intentional in exposing them to other cultures and beyond their native circles.

We seek to Act on issues unique to specific Cultures whilst enabling Cultural Interactions across boundaries. Our Motto is Celebrating Cultures, Connecting Communities.
